1. Both lips = Most important shapes : puh, buh, mm, ww, oo - hold for at least 2 frames
pie    /p/
bye    /b/
my    /m/
way    /w/

2. Lips and Teeth = next important : ff, vv
fear    /f/
veer    /v/

3. Tongue tip and Teeth : th
thaw    /th/

4. Tongue tip and ridge behind teeth : teh, duh, ess, zz, en, el
two    /t/
due    /d/
sue    /s/
zoo    /z/
new    /n/
low    /l/

5. Tongue blade and palate : sh, j, ch, r, y
rush    /sh/
rouge    /j/
rich    /ch/
ridge    /j/
raw    /r/
yaw    /y/

6. tongue back and top of the throat = You can almost ignore, just use jaw movement : k, g, ng
rack    /k/
rag    /g/
rang    /ng/

7. vocal chords - ignore : h
